THE LORE

The tales of N.T.P.D go way back.

It began with a simple mutual love for food and science. Whether it was a class project or a labsession, we all shared with one another our passion.

Years later, as we approached our senior year of high school, we decided to elevate home cooking to new heights. Reuniting around the same flame that first brought us together, it felt as though no time hadpassed.

We set our sights on an ambitious goal: creating a tasting menu, the epitome of fine dining experiences.The true purpose of this highly anticipated project will be disclosed in due time.

This endeavor showcases our current prowess in culinary arts. Drawing from our diverse backgrounds—including professional kitchen experience and advanced scientific knowledge—we ensure aunique blend of skills and perspectives.

NORO is excited to share our creations and infuse the world of cuisine with fresh, innovative ideas.

OUR MISSION

Apart from providing a luxurious private dining service mentioned previously, we set our eyes on a much more noble cause - to make every meal zero waste.

Each year, a staggering 2.5 billion tons of food are wasted globally. If we could salvage even 10-20% of this, our world would become significantly more sustainable. This challenge has become our driving force.

With that in mind, here at NORO, we are committed to making every dish zero or close to minimal waste - utilizing every last bit of food scraps.

All of our generated revenue will be directed into charitable and research purposes. We aim to help provide more research opportunities for students and youth scientists.ts.

Alongside NORO’s private dining reservations, there will be weekly informative posts of ways one can use up food scraps which surprisingly are full of awesomeness.

RESEARCHER + CHEF - KHUC DANG DUY ANH

Chef Duy Anh brings a unique blend of culinary passion and scientific acumen to the kitchen. His impressive academic background is marked by numerous accolades, including awards from prestigious scientific competitions and a coveted internship as a research assistant at renowned universities.

This scientific rigor translates seamlessly into Duy Anh's approach to cooking. He applies the same methodical, analytical mindset to his culinary creations that he does to his scientific research, resulting in dishes that are not only delicious but also innovative and precisely executed.

PASTRY CHEF - NGUYEN TUAN THANH
